Our Services

Assisted Living & Personal Care

We provide personalized support for activities of daily living (ADLs), including assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, and mobility. Our licensed caregiving staff are on site around the clock, and individualized care plans are reviewed regularly to align services with each resident's changing needs.

  • 24/7 on-site caregiving staff
  • Individualized care plans
  • Incontinence management
  • Transportation arrangement

Meal Preparation & Dining Services

Residents enjoy chef-prepared meals served in a family-style dining setting. We focus on nutritious, home-style cooking and accommodate dietary restrictions and special needs to ensure health and enjoyment at every meal.

  • Three healthy, daily meals and snacks
  • Specialized diet accommodations
  • Hydration monitoring

Faith-Based Care & Activities

Our faith-based foundation is woven into all we do, promoting spiritual well-being. We offer regular in-home worship opportunities and activities designed to enrich the mind and spirit.

  • Daily wellness and exercise programs
  • Social gatherings and enrichment activities
  • In-home Bible studies and worship services

Medication Management & Health Monitoring

Safety and wellness are our top priorities. We coordinate with healthcare providers to ensure accurate administration and monitoring of all medications, offering peace of mind to residents and their families.

  • Medication administration and management
  • Coordination with outside healthcare providers
  • Vitals monitoring and health checks
